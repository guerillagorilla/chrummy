    Rules helper

    Current round: Round 1

    Turn flow

    • Draw first: deck or top discard.
    • Lay down required melds, then lay off.
    • Discard one card to end your turn.

    Meld basics

    • Sets: same rank. Runs: same suit, consecutive.
    • Ace is low or high; no wrap-around.
    • At least 50% natural for required melds.

    Wilds + lay offs

    • Wilds: 2s and jokers.
    • After laying down, add cards to any meld.
    • Wilds can be added on lay offs.

    Buying (3+ players)

    • Non-current players can buy the top discard.
    • Cost: discard + one card from the deck.
    • Closest clockwise to discarder wins ties.
